Van Alphen Strawberry plants

Van Alphen Strawberry Plants was originally a small family company which specialised in the production of strawberries. In the early 80’s Van Alphen started with the commercial propagation of strawberry plants. Since 1996 Van Alphen has stopped the production of strawberries and is fully specialised in the propagation of Strawberry Plants.

We are a medium size propagator, specialising in strawberry plants. It is a close company that has all its crop stages in house. Currently we have 20 different varieties in our range. Every year we buy about 280 new plants from the original stock of the NAKT and in 3 years we multiply 20.000.000 plants from it.
